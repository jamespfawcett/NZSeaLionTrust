Get Involved

There are plenty of ways you can help us protect the amazing New Zealand Sea Lion. See below how you can do your part for one of New Zealand’s treasures!

Volunteering & helping out

Getting involved is a great way to contribute to the protection of the New Zealand Sea Lion. Register your interest here and do your part to protect an NZ treasure!

Booking a tour or event

We run events frequently. View them up close and learn all about them from our expert guides. Or have our guides give a talk at your school or workplace!

Donating or Shopping

You can help us protect these amazing creatures by donating or purchasing any items through our shop. Absolutely any amount is appreciated!

Becoming a friend of the trust

Join the NZ Sea Lion Trust team as a member, support our education and research projects and enjoy exclusive benefits. You will receive our newsletters with all the latest news and updates and be kept up to date about our AGM and other events. Membership for an individual is a mere $20 a year. Visit our shop to buy a membership for yourself or as a present.
